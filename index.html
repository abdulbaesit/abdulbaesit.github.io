I am a Computer Science student at Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) with a strong interest in cybersecurity, computer networking, and algorithm design. My research focuses on exploring the landscape of SDN, particularly its real-world vulnerabilities and applications in drone communication networks. I am working on protecting SDN architectures from DoS/DDoS attacks, securing communication between controllers and switches through encryption algorithms, and evaluating trust models and authentication mechanisms in SDN environments, while designing algorithms that are efficient in both time and space complexity for practical use.
